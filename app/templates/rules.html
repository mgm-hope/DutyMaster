How custom rules are applied

The allocator first applies the built-in DutyMaster rules, then applies active hard custom rules. If a member of staff fails any hard custom rule, they are removed from the available list for that duty. If every possible assignment would break a rule, the duty is left blank and recorded for manual review instead of forcing a weak allocation. The final fair ordering still uses available periods, duty count, heavy-duty count, and recent allocations.
