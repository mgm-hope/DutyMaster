How custom rules are applied

The allocator first applies the built-in DutyMaster rules, then applies active hard custom rules. If a member of staff fails any hard custom rule, they are removed from the available list for that duty. The final fair ordering still uses available periods, duty count, heavy-duty count, and recent allocations.
